About me

I am a ranged intelligence hero who uses his abilities to cripple his enemies, supported by a seemingly inexhaustible army of creeps. My fearsome abilities can bind entire teams under his might. I am also a formidable jungler and pusher owing to his ability to create creeps.

The deliverance of my ultimate, Black Hole, engulfs an area around me with dark energies and disables enemies caught within it, making me an extremely powerful yet conditional initiator and disabler. My Midnight Pulse consumes sections of enemies' life in a region over time and my Malefice stuns targets. My trademark ability is Demonic Conversion, which splits a creep into three eidolons under my control. If they can land enough attacks on an enemy unit before their expiration, they multiply and are healed to full health.

"Chaos hunts the spark of endless suns, whose light will die in my crushing grasp."
